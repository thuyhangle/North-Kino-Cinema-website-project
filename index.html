ABOUT US

North Kino is a unit under KINO Group - one of the largest multidisciplinary economic groups of Nordic which is presented in 21 countries around the world.

North Kino has opened a unique concept about cinema with the idea of cultureplex - where audiences not only watch movies at international standards cinemas, but also can enjoy diversified cuisine, buy products related to entertainment & culture, create experience Beyond Movies with advanced cinema technology such as:

  • 4DX : World’s First Five Senses Cinema Screen
  • ScreenX: first 270-degree movie theater in the world
  • Starium: World’s Biggest Screen Officially Recognized by Guinness.
